ABOUT ME

Hi there :) I am Shivam (Shivam Gupta), I'm a Graphic Designer, Animator and 3D artist living in INDIA (Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow). I began studying Animation to work digitally in 2017. Since then, I have been working with Illustrator, Photoshop, Corel Draw, Premiere, After Effects, Lumion, 3DS Max, as well as Maya and Zbrush to create my digital portfolio. I'm passionate about Logo design and 3D modeling plus animation. I love to learn and experiment with new design techniques and styles. The best place to find my art is my Instagram!

TBH For me, design is not only giving aesthetic value to a product or service but developing meaningful effective solutions that can be understood and accessed equally by everyone. Words like dynamic, technical, elegant, creative, classy, innovation, drive my design thinking. My basic concern has been, user-centric, ergonomics, attention to detail, rendering, conceptualization, 3D and prototyping. Though I like all aspects of design but 3D, rendering and prototyping are my favorite, that is why I love to travel and be in nature. I also enjoy photography and playing video games with friends!

Also available for freelance work, private commissions and other creative fields. Feel free to drop a messege or email me @27shivamgupta@gmail.com to socially connect more you can find me on Whatsapp, Instagram also given links at the bottom of my page.
